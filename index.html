How to Build the Full Year

Export a Trial Balance from Tally for the period you want, then click Load TB file in the top bar. The dashboard reads the date range and files it automatically:
📅 Monthly
A 1-month TB (e.g. 1-Apr-26 to 30-Apr-26). Upload all 12 — Apr-26 through Mar-27 — for the full year, viewable month-by-month or year-to-date.
📊 Quarterly
A 3-month TB (e.g. 1-Apr-26 to 30-Jun-26). Switch the view to Quarterly and upload Q1–Q4 TBs for quarterly analysis.
🗓️ Yearly
A full-year TB (e.g. 1-Apr-26 to 31-Mar-27). Switch to Yearly and upload the annual TB for year-end analysis.
